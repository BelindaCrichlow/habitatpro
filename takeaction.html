WHY HABITAT PRO ASSOCIATION?

According to World Directory of Minorities and Indigenous Peoples, there are 51 various indigenous peoples in Peru. Indigenous groups comprise 45% of the population and all of them suffer from inequality at different levels. New generations reject their culture in order to belong to a society. Habitat Pro Association has ongoing projects that empower indigenous women and youth in Peru and help preserving ancient indigenous cultures.
